About Us

Our collaborative project, the Solar Panel Fault Detection System, led by students Harsh Aswar, Himanshu Bendale, Parth Desai, and Hitesh Bamb under Dr. Chetan Aher's guidance, offers an advanced solution for accurately identifying and diagnosing faults in solar panels. By integrating cutting-edge technologies like machine learning and real-time monitoring, our system optimizes solar power systems, minimizing downtime and maximizing energy output.
